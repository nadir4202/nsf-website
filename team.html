About the Founder

Nadir is a seasoned entrepreneur with over 14 years of experience in the iGaming industry. His strengths lie in building and managing teams, bringing people together, promoting healthy competition, and forging sustainable business relationships.

After studying foreign languages and international business management in Scotland and France (2006–2010), Nadir began his career in Malta — joining Betclic Group for 6 years as Head of International VIP, followed by Swedish company VeraJohn where he led CRM and data strategy. He has since become a key strategic partner with Betsson, one of the industry's leading operators.

In 2017, Nadir launched NSF Together Investment Ltd, developing deep expertise in marketing strategy, brand development, business planning, and digital customer growth across five verticals.
